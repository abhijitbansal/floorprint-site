What it does

Scan in one walk

Walls, doors, and windows appear live as you move, with a 0–100 quality score when you finish.

Real 2D floor plans

Accurate dimensions, metric or imperial, with on-canvas editing — move, straighten, merge, undo.

Interactive 3D

Color-coded surfaces, recognized furniture, tap-to-inspect dimensions, adjustable ceiling and window heights.

Pro exports

PDF, DXF, STEP (real CAD solids with door/window cutouts), USDZ for AR, and GLB.

Private by architecture

Your home's layout stays on your device. No uploads, no analytics, no tracking.

Mac companion

Open, edit, and export saved plans on your Mac — or draft plans from scratch in the Layout editor.
