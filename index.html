Floorprint

LiDAR scan to CAD floor plan — all on your iPhone.

Floorprint turns a 60-second walk around a room into an accurate, editable floor plan — and exports it in the formats professionals actually use. Scanning, geometry processing, and every export run entirely on your iPhone. No accounts. No cloud. No subscription.

  • Scan in one walkWalls, doors, and windows appear live as you move, with a 0–100 quality score when you finish.
  • Real 2D floor plansAccurate dimensions, metric or imperial, with on-canvas editing — move, straighten, merge, undo.
  • Interactive 3DColor-coded surfaces, recognized furniture, tap-to-inspect dimensions, adjustable ceiling and window heights.
  • Pro exportsPDF, DXF, STEP (real CAD solids with door/window cutouts), USDZ for AR, and GLB.
  • Private by architectureYour home's layout stays on your device. No uploads, no analytics, no tracking.
  • Mac companionOpen, edit, and export saved plans on your Mac — or draft plans from scratch.
