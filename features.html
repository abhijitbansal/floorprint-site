Floorprint · features

Every room you scan,
yours — on every device.

iPhone + iPad LiDAR scanning, a Mac companion, and a mini-CAD — free, no accounts, processing never leaves the device. Rose monospace lines mark what Polycam, magicplan, RoomScan Pro, or CubiCasa charge for. Gold badges are the newest additions.

clips recorded on the iPhone 17 Pro simulator with seeded plans — scanning itself needs LiDAR hardware

Capture

LiDAR Room Scanning

Walk the room; walls, doors, windows outline live with counts, confidence warnings, and an elapsed timer. Optional GPS address tagging.

Scan Quality Gate

0–100 score after every scan: confidence breakdown, wall-closure check, surface counts, rescan-or-continue.

Scan Post-Processing

Configurable wall straightening, corner snapping, duplicate-wall merging — tuned in Settings.

NEW

Multi-Room Merge

Scan room by room; align each new scan by hand — live drag, 90°/5° rotate, one-tap endpoint snap — then merge into one floor.

auto-stitch is the #1 complaint about every rival; RoomScan loses doors on join
NEW

Multi-Floor Plans

One plan, many floors: switcher in the control bar, scan straight into a new floor, archives and reports carry every floor.

no competitor does whole-house reliably at any price

Plan & Edit

2D Architectural Plans

Section-cut wall rendering, door arcs and window panes, dimension overlays in metric or feet-inches, compass, scale bar.

2D Edit Mode

Select, move, straighten, merge corners, merge walls, add, delete — full undo/redo, per-wall colors, snap-to-axis HUD.

NEW

Measure & Annotate

Tap-tap dimension lines with draggable endpoints, free-text notes, room labels with auto-computed areas (Bedroom · 14.2 m²).

Polycam: Business tier · RoomScan: Apple Pencil only
NEW

Furniture Layer

Scan-detected furniture renders as architectural footprints; place more from a 16-category catalog — move, rotate, resize, toggle.

furniture/fixture layers are paid in all five competitors

Layout Editor (Mac mini-CAD)

Draft plans with no scan: constraint solver (lengths, H/V, parallel, perpendicular, angles), room wizard ("Kitchen 4x4 east of Living"), trim/extend, fillet/chamfer, typed dimensions, crash recovery.

drafts export through every pipeline a scan can

3D

3D Model Viewer

SceneKit walkthrough with color-coded surfaces, custom furniture meshes, top-view snap, and 3D edit (move/resize/delete objects).

Object Inspector

Tap any surface or furnishing: dimensions, surface area, confidence, world position, inferred sub-type ("L-Shaped Sofa").

UPDATED

Parametric 3D

Adjustable ceiling/door/window heights with per-wall overrides, flat or vaulted ceilings, mitered corners, ray-traced-look mode — now with placed furniture as scaled boxes.

Export — all free, all local

PDF + DXF

A4 architectural drawing (now with annotations + furniture) and DXF R12 with WALLS/DOORS/WINDOWS/ANNOTATIONS/FURNITURE layers.

DXF: paid in Polycam, magicplan, CubiCasa (+$50/scan)
NEW

Plan Report

Multi-page PDF: cover with summary stats and a rendered plan, then room / wall / door-window schedules — a section per floor.

CubiCasa: per-report fee · magicplan Report tier: $39.99/mo

USDZ + GLB + STEP

AR Quick Look, web-ready glTF, and ISO 10303-21 B-Rep STEP with mitered corners and door/window cutouts — real CAD geometry, not a mesh dump.

3D exports: Business/paid tiers everywhere; STEP almost nowhere

.floorplan Archives

Zip-based plan exchange via share sheet, Files, AirDrop — iOS ↔ macOS, floors included, no cloud required.

Platform

macOS Companion

Open, edit (2D + parametric), and export saved plans on the Mac; import .floorplan archives; Layout editor lives here.

NEW

Native iPad

Sidebar split view (no compatibility mode), keyboard shortcuts (⌘N ⌘E ⌘1–4 ⌘Z), trackpad hover, LiDAR scanning on iPad Pro.

NEW

iCloud Sync — opt-in

One Settings switch moves the library into iCloud Drive; plans appear on iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Off again = everything back on-device. Availability-gated, resumable migration.

rivals: subscriptions or per-project fees for cross-device
NEW

Dark Mode & Accessibility

Scheme-aware plan rendering, VoiceOver-labeled tools and cards, Dynamic Type, Reduce Motion/Transparency, Increase Contrast, non-color selection cues.
